CANlab NiiVue sample — emotionreg t-map

One-sample t-test on the CANlab emotionreg sample dataset, displayed as a web orthviews. The hot (warm) lobe shows positive t-values and the cool (winter) lobe shows negative t-values, overlaid on an anatomical underlay. Use the toolbar to switch between axial, coronal, sagittal, multiplanar, and 3D-render layouts; move the crosshair to read the MNI coordinate and t-value below the canvas.
